Why mixing?

Ethereum isn't anonymous by default. Everyone can see where your Ether is going to or coming from. The key is to hide which address belongs to you. That's what we are here for. By using our service we will break the link between addresses for you!

How does it work?

After recieving your Ether we will send you freshly premixed Ether from our reserve directly to the address you've given to us. Your old Ether will then be added to the reserve. In order to not recieve your old Ether back next time you have to put in your mixing code you recieved on your first order. For further information please take a look at our FAQ.

What are your fees?

0.001 Ether fixed per generated address and less than 2% on the remaining amount (minus the fixed fee). So if you would send us 1.001 Ether you would recieve at least 0.98 Ether (minus 0.001ETH fixed and minus less than 2%/0.02ETH variable). Please take a look at our fee calculator to make things easier.
